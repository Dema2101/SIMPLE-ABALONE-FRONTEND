ABOUT US

The Birth of Simple Abalone

In the vibrant port city of Rijeka, Croatia, there lived a young woman named Danijela. From her apartment overlooking the Adriatic Sea, she found solace in the rhythmic sounds of waves crashing against the shore. Danijela's fascination with the ocean ran deep, and her heart belonged to its treasures. Among them, she found herself captivated by the mesmerizing beauty of abalone shells. Their shimmering colors and unique patterns inspired her creativity.
Fuelled by her love for the sea and a desire to share its wonders with the world, Danijela embarked on a journey of creation. With unwavering determination, she established her own company—Simple Abalone.
Armed with nothing but her creativity and a deep respect for nature, Danijela set out to make her dream a reality. She scoured the shores for the finest abalone shells, carefully selecting only those that met her high standards.
